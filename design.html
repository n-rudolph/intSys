Functionality

PicPlace is a social network that aims to improve the user experience of travelers when arriving to a new place. For this PicPlace relies on a community of travelers that uploads pictures from different POIs and indicate the spot from where it was taken. This way other user can view this images and the spots from where they were taken and experience the city/place in a different way. Its main focus is to help travelers find great places to take memories of the different POIs.

To improve even more the user experience during a trip, PicPlace let you create tours of a city using different photo spots. This way the traveller do not waste time in finding the spots, the application will guide them directly to them, creating the most convenient tour for the selected spots. This tours can be either public or private. By making them public anyone can see it in the owner’s profile and use it for their own visit.

In matters of UX, PicPlace is aimed at travelers who might not have technical experience in photography and also those who do not have much time to find the best spot to take a picture of a POI. This is why the application would run in a user-friendly, simple-to-use environment, improving trips for families, couples, solo travellers, friends, and any person who likes travelling and taking photos. Apart from the easiness of usage, the application would include the main information of the POI, letting the users to get to know a bit better the place they are visiting. About social aspects, PicPlace would offer a simple way to share their interactions within the application into another well known social networks.
