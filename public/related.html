Related Work

Currently there is no solution for the problems and needs that PicPlace covers.
Anyway, some tools exist but they only offer partial solutions for the travelers' needs.

On one hand, tools such as Google Maps or similar allow the user to find POIs. The main advantages rely on the UX, community, devices availability and information about POIs. Unfortunately, these apps do not intend to work as social networks and are not focused on taking and sharing photos.

On the other hand, The Photographer's Ephemeris or Improve Photography, applications focused on photography do not address other needs and problems of the user. The first one, requires technical knowledge about photography and has a complicated UI. The second one, instead, has a simpler UI but it does not have a good UX. The application is focused on photo spots, but does not work as a social network and the number of spots for a POI is limited.

instead, is meant to be simple, with no technical knowledge required, multiplatform and to work as a social tool. The target is anyone who wants to travel and share experiences, no matter the age, social class or type of trip.
